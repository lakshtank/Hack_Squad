Frequently Asked Questions

How does SkillSwap work?

SkillSwap connects people who want to learn specific skills with those who can teach them. You can browse available skills, connect with teachers, and start learning immediately.

Is SkillSwap free to use?

Yes! SkillSwap is completely free to use. We believe in making learning accessible to everyone.

How do I become a teacher?

Anyone can become a teacher on SkillSwap. Simply create an account, add your skills, and start connecting with learners.

What skills can I learn?

You can learn virtually any skill on SkillSwap - from languages and music to coding and cooking. The possibilities are endless!
